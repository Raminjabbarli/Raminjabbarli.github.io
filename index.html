About

Ramin Jabbarli is a PhD candidate in Sociology at the University of Washington. His research examines how ritual practices shape national belonging and interethnic solidarity across dominant and minority groups with a comparative focus on Iran and Turkey. He uses mixed methods that combine archival and newspaper research, text analysis, and statistical modeling of multi-wave survey data.

Languages: English (Fluent), Azerbaijani (Native), Turkish (Fluent), Persian/Farsi (Fluent)
